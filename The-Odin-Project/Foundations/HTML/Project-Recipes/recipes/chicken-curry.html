Chicken Curry

Description

This chicken curry recipe delivers a rich, flavorful, and aromatic dish that's surprisingly easy to make. Featuring tender pieces of chicken simmered in a luscious, spiced sauce, it's perfect served with fluffy rice or warm naan bread. It balances the warmth of spices with a creamy texture, making it a crowd-pleaser for any meal.

You can adjust the spice level to your preference, making it mild for a gentle warmth or adding more chili for a fiery kick. This recipe is versatile and a fantastic way to enjoy the comforting flavors of South Asian cuisine at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/4 - 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (to taste)
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can full-fat coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Cooked rice or naan, for serving

Steps

  1. **Saut√© Aromatics:** Heat oil or ghee in a large skillet or pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened and lightly golden, about 5-7 minutes. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  2. **Add Spices:** Stir in cumin, coriander, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until the spices are aromatic.
  3. **Brown Chicken:** Add chicken pieces to the pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. **Simmer Curry:** Stir in diced tomatoes (with their juice), coconut milk, and chicken broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender, and the sauce has thickened slightly. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. **Season and Serve:** Season the curry with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro. Serve hot with cooked rice or warm naan bread.
