Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce (or vegetable sauce), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's a comforting and hearty meal, perfect for family gatherings or a satisfying weeknight dinner. The magic happens as it bakes, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully.

While it might seem intimidating, making lasagna from scratch is a rewarding experience. This recipe simplifies the process while delivering an authentic, delicious result that will surely impress. Enjoy the rich textures and savory flavors of this timeless dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional, to cut acidity)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Pinch of nutmeg (optional)
  • 1 pound (4 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. **Prepare the Meat Sauce:** In a large skillet or Dutch oven, cook ground beef (or sausage) and onion over medium heat until meat is browned and onion is tender. Drain off excess fat. Stir in garlic and cook for 1 minute more. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar (if using), basil, fennel seeds (if using), Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for at least 30 minutes (longer is better for flavor development), stirring occasionally.
  2. **Cook Noodles:** While sauce simmers, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
  3. **Prepare Ricotta Mixture:** In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and nutmeg (if using). Mix well.
  4. **Assemble Lasagna:** Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread about 1/2 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with about 1 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese, then about 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce. Repeat layers: noodles, remaining ricotta, mozzarella, meat sauce. Top with remaining noodles, then the rest of the meat sauce, and finally, a generous layer of mozzarella and a sprinkle of Parmesan.
  5. **Bake:** Cover the baking dish loosely with aluminum foil (you can spray the underside of the foil with cooking spray to prevent cheese from sticking). Bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until golden brown and bubbly. Let stand for 10-15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
